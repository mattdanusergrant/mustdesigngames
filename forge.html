SIGIL TACTICS

Five blank recruits. Every card you cast does its thing — then tucks behind the unit as a level in its class. Two of one glyph and the recruit becomes that hero.

How it works
• On your turn, activate one of your lowest-level units (fewest tucked cards, dashed gold).
• It may move once, and must cast one card — no plain attacks. The card resolves and tucks behind it (+1 level in that glyph).
• Hit from the side (×1.5) or rear (×2) of a unit's facing for bonus damage.
• Stack 2 of one glyph on a unit → it attunes: gains that hero's passive + a stat boost.
• Wipe out all 5 enemy units to win.
